## Job Title
Java J2EE Senior Tech Lead

## Location
Chennai or Noida

## Client Industry
Multinational Technology Provider Company

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Lead and mentor a team of developers, fostering a collaborative and productive work environment.
- Design, develop, and deploy enterprise-level applications using Java J2EE, Microservices, and Spring Boot.
- Oversee the architecture and technical design of projects, ensuring adherence to best practices and industry standards.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather requirements, define project scope, and deliver solutions on time.
- Conduct code reviews, provide constructive feedback, and implement improvements to enhance code quality and maintainability.
- Troubleshoot and resolve complex technical issues, ensuring the stability and reliability of applications.
- Stay updated with the latest advancements in Java J2EE and related technologies, advocating for continuous improvement and innovation.

## Skill Requirements
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
- 5-7 years of experience in Java J2EE development, with a strong focus on Microservices and Spring Boot.
- Proven experience in leading technical teams and delivering successful projects.
- Proficiency in designing and implementing scalable, high-performance applications.
- Strong understanding of software development methodologies, including Agile and Scrum.
- Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to troubleshoot complex issues.
- Effective communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to collaborate with diverse teams.

## Other Requirements
- Experience with cloud platforms such as AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud.
- Knowledge of containerization technologies like Docker and Kubernetes.
- Familiarity with DevOps practices and tools for continuous integration and deployment.
- Certification in Java or related technologies is a plus.
