## Job Title
Pega Senior Technical Lead

## Location
Madurai, India

## Client Industry
Technology Solutions (Multinational Technology Provider)

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Lead, mentor, and manage a team of Pega technical professionals through all phases of application development, customization, and maintenance.
- Collaborate closely with business stakeholders to elicit requirements and translate them into scalable technical solutions utilizing Pega technology.
- Oversee the end-to-end implementation of Pega applications, ensuring timely and quality delivery in accordance with organizational standards.
- Provide technical guidance, coaching, and support to team members, fostering a culture of continuous improvement and best practice adoption.
- Troubleshoot and resolve complex technical issues, developing innovative solutions to enhance application performance and reliability.
- Partner with cross-functional teams to ensure seamless integration of Pega applications with existing enterprise systems and technologies.
- Monitor project progress, manage risks, and ensure the achievement of project milestones and deliverables.
- Maintain comprehensive documentation and ensure adherence to compliance, security, and quality standards.

## Skill Requirements
- Proven proficiency in Pega application development, including customization and maintenance.
- In-depth understanding of Pega platform features such as case management, decision strategies, and UI/UX development.
- Demonstrated experience in leading technical teams for Pega application implementation and support projects.
- Strong problem-solving skills with a track record of analyzing and resolving technical challenges in Pega environments.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills to effectively collaborate with stakeholders and team members.
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field (preferred).
- Pega Certified System Architect (CSA) or Pega Certified Senior System Architect (CSSA) certification (preferred, but not mandatory).

## Other Requirements
- Experience working in a global, multicultural environment.
- Exposure to integrating Pega with third-party and legacy systems.
- Familiarity with Agile methodologies and DevOps practices.
