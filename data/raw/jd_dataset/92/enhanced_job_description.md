## Job Title
Embedded Engineer

## Location
N/A

## Client Industry
Multinational Technology Provider

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Design and develop embedded software for various applications.
- Conduct thorough testing and debugging of embedded systems to ensure optimal functionality.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to integrate embedded solutions into larger systems.
- Participate in code reviews and contribute to continuous improvement of software quality.
- Document software designs, test plans, and procedures for future reference.
- Stay updated with the latest industry trends and technologies to implement innovative solutions.
- Provide technical support and troubleshoot issues related to embedded systems.

## Skill Requirements
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Science, or a related field.
- Strong proficiency in C/C++ programming languages.
- Experience with embedded systems development and real-time operating systems (RTOS).
- Familiarity with hardware-software integration and debugging tools.
- Knowledge of communication protocols such as I2C, SPI, UART, etc.
- Ability to work effectively in a collaborative team environment.
- Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.

## Other Requirements
- Experience with ARM microcontrollers and development platforms.
- Understanding of software development lifecycle and agile methodologies.
- Certification in embedded systems or related technologies.
- Knowledge of Linux kernel programming and device drivers.
