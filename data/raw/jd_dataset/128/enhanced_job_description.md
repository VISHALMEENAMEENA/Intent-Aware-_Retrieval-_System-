## Detailed Responsibilities
- Design, develop, and maintain robust and scalable Java J2EE applications using Spring Boot.
- Collaborate with product managers, architects, and other developers to gather requirements and translate them into technical specifications.
- Perform code reviews and provide constructive feedback to team members to ensure code quality and best practices.

- Implement and maintain automated testing frameworks to enhance the stability of applications.
- Stay up-to-date with emerging technologies and industry trends to continuously improve development processes and solutions.

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
- 2-5 years of experience in Java J2EE development, with a strong focus on Spring Boot.
- Proficiency in web technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and RESTful APIs.
- Strong understanding of software development methodologies, including Agile and DevOps practices.
- Experience with database technologies (SQL, NoSQL) and ORM frameworks (Hibernate, JPA).
- Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to work effectively in a team environment.

## Other Requirements

- Knowledge of containerization technologies (Docker, Kubernetes) would be advantageous.
- Experience with CI/CD tools (Jenkins, GitLab CI) is preferred.
