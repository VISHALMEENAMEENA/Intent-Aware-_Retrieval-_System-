## Job Title
Senior Performance Test Lead

## Location
N/A

## Client Industry
Information Technology — Multinational Technology Provider

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Lead the planning, design, and execution of comprehensive performance testing strategies for various software applications.
- Develop, maintain, and enhance performance test scripts and scenarios utilizing industry-standard tools such as JMeter and LoadRunner.
- Analyze performance test results to identify bottlenecks, risks, and opportunities for optimization, offering actionable recommendations.
- Collaborate with architects, developers, and product teams to ensure that performance requirements are addressed from design through deployment.
- Mentor and guide junior team members, fostering a culture of technical excellence and continuous improvement in performance testing practices.
- Document test processes, results, and recommendations clearly for diverse stakeholders, ensuring transparency and accountability.
- Stay abreast of the latest trends, tools, and methodologies in performance engineering, integrating best practices into daily operations.
- Drive continuous process improvements for performance testing to enhance efficiency, accuracy, and value delivered to clients.

## Skill Requirements
- Proven expertise in performance testing methodologies, with hands-on experience using JMeter and LoadRunner.
- Demonstrated ability to create, execute, and maintain complex performance test scripts and scenarios.
- Strong knowledge of performance monitoring, profiling, and tuning techniques for enterprise applications.
- Excellent analytical skills with the ability to diagnose and resolve performance issues effectively.
- Exceptional communication and collaboration skills, with experience working in multi-disciplinary, agile teams.
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Engineering, or a related field.
- Relevant certifications in performance testing tools (e.g., JMeter, LoadRunner) are highly desirable.

## Other Requirements
- Experience with cloud-based performance testing and CI/CD integration is a plus.
- Exposure to diverse technology stacks, including web, mobile, and microservices architectures, is advantageous.
- Familiarity with scripting languages (e.g., Python, Shell) for automation is an added benefit.
- Prior experience in a client-facing or consulting role is preferred.
