- Implement and enforce best practices for code quality, security, and performance optimization across all development activities, ensuring secure coding standards and performance tuning.

- Stay abreast of emerging technologies and industry trends in Java, Node.js, microservices, AWS, and Gen AI, fostering a culture of continuous learning and innovation within the team.

- Ensure projects adhere to established standards, compliance requirements, and align with HCLTech’s ESG and sustainability objectives.
