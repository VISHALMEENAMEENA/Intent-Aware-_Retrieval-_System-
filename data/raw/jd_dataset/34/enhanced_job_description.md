## Job Title
Trainee – Voice Operations (DPO)

## Location
DPO-North Ireland, United Kingdom

## Client Industry
Information Technology / Technology Solutions

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Manage inbound and outbound customer calls, utilizing advanced voice modulation and accent techniques to ensure clear, professional communication.
- Address customer inquiries and provide accurate information aligned with company standards.
- Resolve customer issues and complaints promptly, using operational management principles to maintain high satisfaction levels.
- Adhere to established processes, protocols, and SLAs to deliver consistent, high-quality customer service.
- Participate in ongoing training programs to enhance product knowledge, communication, and service delivery skills.
- Actively engage in self-directed learning to stay updated with industry best practices and company offerings.
- Collect customer feedback and contribute ideas for continuous process and service improvement.
- Comply with all company policies, including strict adherence to confidentiality and ethical guidelines.

## Skill Requirements
**Essential:**
- Basic understanding of voice and accent modulation techniques.
- Familiarity with customer service principles and operational management.
- Strong verbal communication skills with a focus on clarity, professionalism, and active listening.
- Demonstrated learning agility with a drive to acquire new skills and knowledge.
- Ability to collaborate effectively with customers and team members.

**Education & Experience:**
- No prior experience required; fresh graduates are encouraged to apply.
- Educational background in any discipline. Additional coursework in communication, business, or related fields is a plus.

## Other Requirements
- Certifications in customer service or voice training are advantageous but not mandatory.
- Multilingual abilities or experience working in multicultural environments will be seen as an asset.
