## Job Title
AWS Senior Data Lead

## Location
Austria

## Client Industry
Information Technology & Services (applicable across Financial Services, Manufacturing, Life Sciences & Healthcare, Technology, Telecom & Media, Retail & CPG, and Public Services)

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Provide technical leadership and direction for projects leveraging Amazon Glue, SQL, Python, DevOps practices, and Snowflake.
- Architect, design, and implement robust ETL solutions using Amazon Glue to meet complex data integration requirements.
- Develop, optimize, and maintain high-performance SQL queries and database solutions to support diverse data initiatives.
- Create and enhance scalable Python applications to automate workflows, improve data processes, and support analytics.
- Implement and manage DevOps solutions, ensuring seamless continuous integration, delivery, and deployment of data solutions.
- Design and optimize Snowflake data warehouses for high-performance analytics, reporting, and business intelligence.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including business analysts, engineers, and product managers, to align solutions with strategic business goals.
- Foster technical innovation, champion best practices, and drive process improvements across data engineering initiatives.
- Provide mentorship and guidance to junior team members, promoting a culture of learning and technical excellence.
- Ensure compliance with organizational standards, data governance, and security requirements.

## Skill Requirements
- Proficiency in Amazon Glue for ETL development and data integration.
- Advanced SQL skills for database management, optimization, and querying.
- Strong expertise in Python for automation and application development.
- Hands-on experience with DevOps tools and practices for continuous integration and delivery.
- In-depth knowledge of Snowflake for cloud-based data warehousing and analytics.
- Proven experience in leading technical teams and managing complex data projects.
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and troubleshooting skills.
- Strong communication and stakeholder management abilities.
- Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Engineering, or a related discipline.
- Relevant certifications in Amazon Glue, Snowflake, Python, SQL, or DevOps (preferred).

## Other Requirements
- Experience working in Agile-Scrum environments, particularly within digital transformation projects.
- Additional certifications in AWS, DevOps, or data engineering technologies are advantageous.
- Exposure to large-scale, multinational data environments is a plus.
- Multilingual proficiency is an asset, especially in English and German.
