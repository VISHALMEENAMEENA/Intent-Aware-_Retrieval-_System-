## Job Title
Senior Development Lead

## Location
Romania

## Client Industry
Information Technology/Technology Solutions

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Lead the end-to-end design, development, and implementation of Java, Microservices, and React.js-based solutions for global clients.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams—including product managers, architects, and QA engineers—to deliver complex projects on time and within scope.
- Mentor and guide team members, fostering best practices in coding standards, architecture, and agile methodologies.
- Conduct code reviews and enforce adherence to high-quality standards and best practices across all project phases.
- Troubleshoot and resolve complex technical challenges in backend and frontend applications, ensuring seamless system integration.
- Stay abreast of emerging technologies and industry trends, proactively recommending enhancements to existing systems and processes.
- Drive continuous improvement in development workflows, CI/CD pipelines, and DevOps practices.
- Ensure comprehensive documentation throughout all phases of software development.
- Act as a key technical stakeholder in project planning, estimation, and risk management activities.

## Skill Requirements
- Proven expertise in Java programming and the Spring framework.
- Extensive experience in designing and developing scalable microservices architecture.
- Proficient in frontend development using React.js, with a solid understanding of modern JavaScript frameworks.
- Strong knowledge of RESTful APIs, web services, and backend integration.
- Experience with containerization technologies such as Docker and Kubernetes.
- Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines, version control systems, and DevOps best practices.
- Excellent problem-solving skills, with the ability to analyze and resolve technical issues efficiently.
- Exceptional communication and leadership skills, enabling effective team guidance and stakeholder management.
- Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.

## Other Requirements
- Relevant certifications in Java, Microservices, or React.js are highly advantageous.
- Experience working in a multinational, client-facing environment.
- Exposure to cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP) is a plus.
- Agile/Scrum certification is desirable.
