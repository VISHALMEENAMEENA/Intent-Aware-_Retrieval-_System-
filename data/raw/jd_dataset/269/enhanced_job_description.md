## Job Title
React Developer (Next.js & Tailwind CSS)

## Location
N/A

## Client Industry
Information Technology / Technology Solutions

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Develop, implement, and maintain web applications using React.js, Next.js, and Tailwind CSS.
- Collaborate with UX/UI designers to translate designs into responsive, accessible user interfaces.
- Optimize applications for maximum speed, scalability, and performance across devices and browsers.
- Integrate APIs and third-party services, ensuring seamless functionality and data flow.
- Participate in code reviews, contribute to architectural decisions, and promote coding standards.
- Troubleshoot, debug, and resolve application issues in a timely manner.
- Work within Agile/Scrum teams to deliver high-quality software solutions and iterative improvements.
- Document technical specifications, processes, and project progress as required.
- Stay updated with industry trends, emerging technologies, and best practices in front-end development.

## Skill Requirements
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field (or equivalent experience).
- Proven experience in React.js development with proficiency in Next.js and Tailwind CSS.
- Strong understanding of JavaScript, ES6+, HTML5, and CSS3 fundamentals.
- Experience with responsive design and cross-browser compatibility.
- Familiarity with RESTful APIs and asynchronous data handling.
- Knowledge of version control systems such as Git.
- Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Strong communication and teamwork abilities.

## Other Requirements
- Experience with TypeScript and state management libraries (Redux, Context API).
- Exposure to CI/CD pipelines and DevOps practices.
- Familiarity with testing frameworks (Jest, React Testing Library).
- Understanding of accessibility standards (WCAG, ARIA).
- Previous experience working with multinational or enterprise-scale projects.
