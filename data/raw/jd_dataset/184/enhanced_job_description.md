## Job Title
People Analytics Specialist

## Location
N/A

## Client Industry
Multinational Technology Provider

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Conduct in-depth people analytics to provide actionable insights on workforce trends and behaviors.
- Design and implement hypothesis tests to validate HR strategies and interventions.
- Utilize NLP techniques to analyze employee feedback and sentiment.
- Perform Organizational Network Analysis (ONA) to understand and optimize communication and collaboration within the company.
- Collaborate with HR and business leaders to identify key areas for improvement and drive data-informed decision-making.
- Develop and maintain dashboards and reports to track HR metrics and KPIs.
- Stay abreast of the latest developments in people analytics and incorporate best practices into the company's HR processes.

## Skill Requirements
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Data Science, Statistics, Human Resources, or a related field.
- Proven experience in people analytics, with a strong understanding of HR processes and metrics.
- Proficiency in hypothesis testing and statistical analysis.
- Experience with NLP tools and techniques.
- Familiarity with Organizational Network Analysis (ONA) methodologies.
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Excellent communication abilities, with the capacity to present complex data in an understandable manner.
- Proficiency in data visualization tools and software (e.g., Tableau, Power BI).

## Other Requirements
- Experience with machine learning algorithms and predictive modeling.
- Certifications in HR analytics or related fields.
- Knowledge of advanced statistical software (e.g., R, Python).
