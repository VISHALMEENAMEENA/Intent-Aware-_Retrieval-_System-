## Job Title
Senior Mechanical Engineer

## Location
N/A

## Client Industry
Multinational Technology Provider

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Provide technical leadership and significant contributions to product development projects and technology initiatives.
- Contribute to the creation and maintenance of product Design History Files, including requirements documentation, prototyping, design/development records, technical risk mitigation, verification testing, and design validation studies.
- Investigate and develop engineering solutions, evaluate competing technologies, and identify design solutions that meet performance, cost, and manufacturability requirements.
- Develop product verification and validation testing requirements, author testing protocols, and ensure products meet established specifications.
- Employ statistical techniques to ensure exceptional product quality, reliability, and robustness.
- Support internal and external suppliers and manufacturers, including external design and development partners.
- Assist marketing in the creation of technical brochures, training programs, and presentations to customers and the sales force.
- Provide leadership and direction to Engineering Technicians as needed.

## Skill Requirements
- Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering (preferably from reputed institutions like IITs/NITs/Equivalent).
- 5-15 years of related experience, or a minimum of 5 years of experience in any OEM of medical devices with an MS, or 2 years with a PhD.
- Experience in medical device development and/or sustaining engineering efforts utilizing a stage-gate process.
- Experience in handheld electro-mechanical devices is preferred.
- Working knowledge of FDA/EUMDR Design Controls and international standards (e.g., Design Controls, 820-CFR, ISO14971, ISO10993, IEC60601, GMP).
- Proficient in Microsoft Windows Office applications (MS Outlook, MS PowerPoint, MS Project, Visio) and working knowledge of Solidworks/CREO/UG NX.
- Proficient in creating and critically reviewing engineering drawings, tolerancing, and tolerance analysis.
- Knowledge of common manufacturing methodologies, technologies, and materials used in medical devices.

## Other Requirements
- Ability to develop deliverables required to document product design for high-volume manufacturing with high quality.
