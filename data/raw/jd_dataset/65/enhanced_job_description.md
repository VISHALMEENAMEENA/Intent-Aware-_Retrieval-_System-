## Job Title
AWS Solutions Architect

## Location
N/A

## Client Industry
Multinational Technology Provider

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand business requirements and translate them into effective cloud architecture solutions.
- Design and architect AWS-based solutions, ensuring scalability, security, and reliability.
- Provide expert guidance and support during the implementation and migration phases.
- Conduct architecture reviews and performance tuning to optimize cloud environments.
- Develop and deliver technical presentations and demonstrations to stakeholders.
- Keep abreast of the latest AWS services and technologies to drive continuous improvement.
- Document best practices and create architecture blueprints to facilitate knowledge sharing.

## Skill Requirements
- Minimum of 10 years of experience in IT with a focus on cloud architecture and solutions design.
- Extensive hands-on experience with AWS services, including EC2, S3, RDS, Lambda, and VPC.
- Strong understanding of networking, security, and compliance within cloud environments.
- Proven experience in architecting and implementing large-scale, complex cloud solutions.
- AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Professional certification is highly desirable.
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.

## Other Requirements
- Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to effectively present complex solutions to both technical and non-technical stakeholders.
- Proven track record of successful project management and leadership within a fast-paced, dynamic environment.
- Familiarity with DevOps practices and tools, such as CI/CD pipelines and Infrastructure as Code (IaC).
