## Job Title
Technical Lead – Java, Node.js, Microservices

## Location
Canada

## Client Industry
Any Industry

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Lead and manage a team of Java and Node.js developers, providing mentorship, technical direction, and support throughout the project lifecycle.
- Architect, design, and deliver robust microservices-based solutions leveraging Java, Node.js, and AWS, ensuring scalability, reliability, and performance.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams—including product management, QA, and operations—to integrate modules and services seamlessly.
- Drive the adoption of best practices in code quality, performance optimization, security, and DevOps within development teams.
- Oversee technical troubleshooting, root cause analysis, and resolution of complex production and development issues to maintain project timelines and quality standards.
- Stay abreast of emerging technologies and advancements in Java, Node.js, Microservices, and AWS, and advocate for their adoption to foster innovation.
- Ensure all deliverables align with business objectives, compliance standards, and customer requirements.

## Skill Requirements
**Technical Skills:**
- Advanced proficiency in Java and associated frameworks (e.g., Spring Boot).
- Strong hands-on experience in Node.js for backend service development.
- Demonstrable expertise in designing, implementing, and maintaining microservices architectures.
- In-depth knowledge of Amazon Web Services (AWS) including deployment, monitoring, and scaling strategies.
- Solid experience with MySQL or other relational databases.
- Familiarity with CI/CD pipelines, DevOps practices, and modern development tools.

**Qualifications:**
- Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field (or equivalent practical experience).
- 7+ years of professional software development experience, with at least 2 years in a technical leadership role.
- Proven track record in delivering complex, high-quality software solutions on time.

**Essential Attributes:**
- Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Strong verbal and written communication abilities.
- Effective leadership and team management skills.

## Other Requirements
- AWS certifications (such as AWS Certified Solutions Architect or AWS Certified Developer) are a plus.
- Experience working in Agile/Scrum environments.
- Prior experience with containerization technologies (e.g., Docker, Kubernetes) is advantageous.
- Exposure to additional cloud platforms or frontend technologies.
