## Detailed Responsibilities
- Design and develop backend services and APIs to support front-end applications.
- Integrate backend services with front-end interfaces, ensuring seamless functionality.
- Build and maintain DevOps pipelines for continuous integration and deployment.
- Design and implement database schemas for complex enterprise applications.
- Deploy applications on hyper-scalers, ensuring optimal performance and scalability.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship new features.
- Troubleshoot and resolve issues related to application performance and deployment.
- Maintain code quality, organization, and automation.

## Skill Requirements
- Extensive experience in building and deploying full stack applications at scale.
- Proficiency in backend technologies such as Node.js, Python, or Java.
- Strong understanding of front-end technologies like React, Angular, or Vue.js.
- Experience with designing and developing APIs and web services.
- Knowledge of DevOps practices and tools, including CI/CD pipelines.
- Expertise in database design and management (SQL and NoSQL).
- Familiarity with deploying applications on hyper-scalers (AWS, Azure, Google Cloud).
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field.
- Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.

## Other Requirements

- Knowledge of microservices architecture.
- Strong understanding of cloud-native application development.
- Certification in relevant technologies or cloud platforms is a plus.
