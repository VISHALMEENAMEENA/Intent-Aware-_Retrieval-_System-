## Job Title
ServiceNow Developer - Mid Level (General, HRSD, PSM, VA/Portal)

## Location
N/A

## Client Industry
Multinational Technology Provider

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Develop and customize ServiceNow applications and modules, with a focus on General, HRSD, PSM, and VA/Portal.
- Perform platform upgrades and version migrations to ensure the latest features and security enhancements are implemented.
- Design and implement integrations between ServiceNow and third-party systems using APIs and web services.
- Manage and maintain the ServiceNow CMDB and asset management modules.
- Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues, providing timely solutions to ensure platform stability.
- Collaborate with stakeholders to gather requirements, define project scope, and deliver solutions that meet business objectives.
- Create and maintain comprehensive documentation for development activities, configurations, and integrations.
- Stay updated with industry best practices and emerging trends in ServiceNow development.

## Skill Requirements
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
- 6+ years of experience in ServiceNow development.
- Proficiency in General, HRSD, PSM, VA/Portal.
- Expertise in JavaScript, XML, HTML, and CSS for ServiceNow development.
- Experience with platform upgrades and version migrations.
- Ability to design and implement integrations using APIs and web services.
- In-depth knowledge of ServiceNow CMDB and asset management.
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
- Strong communication and stakeholder management skills.
- ServiceNow Certified Implementation Specialist (CIS) certifications.

## Other Requirements
- Experience with Agile development methodologies.
- Familiarity with ITIL processes and best practices.
- Knowledge of additional ServiceNow modules or technologies.
