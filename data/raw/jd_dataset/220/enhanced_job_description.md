## Detailed Responsibilities
- Design, develop, and maintain Python-based applications for GenAI projects, ensuring high performance and scalability.
- Integrate and deploy AI models on cloud platforms (such as AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud), ensuring robust and secure operations.
- Collaborate with data scientists, DevOps engineers, and cross-functional teams to deliver end-to-end AI solutions.
- Optimize code and system architectures for efficient cloud resource utilization and cost-effectiveness.
- Develop, test, and document APIs and microservices to support AI-driven functionalities.
- Troubleshoot, debug, and enhance existing cloud-based AI applications.
- Stay updated with the latest advancements in AI, machine learning, and cloud technologies, and recommend best practices.
- Participate in code reviews, knowledge sharing, and continuous improvement initiatives.
- Ensure compliance with security, privacy, and regulatory requirements in all project deliverables.

- Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
- Proven experience in Python development, with a strong portfolio of cloud-based projects.
- Hands-on expertise with at least one major cloud platform (AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud).
- Solid understanding of AI/ML concepts, frameworks (such as TensorFlow, PyTorch, or Scikit-learn), and model deployment.
- Experience with RESTful APIs, microservices architecture, and containerization (Docker/Kubernetes).
- Proficiency with version control systems (e.g., Git) and CI/CD pipeline implementation.
- Strong analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills.
