## Detailed Responsibilities
- Design, develop, and optimize embedded software solutions using C++ for various platforms.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams including hardware engineers, system architects, and QA to define software requirements and deliverables.
- Participate in code reviews and contribute to the continuous improvement of coding standards and best practices.

- Develop and maintain detailed technical documentation for software modules and projects.
- Participate in the entire software development lifecycle, from concept to deployment and support.
- Ensure compliance with industry standards, security protocols, and client-specific requirements.

## Skill Requirements
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Electronics, Electrical Engineering, or a related field.
- Minimum of 2 years of hands-on experience in C++ development for embedded systems.
- Strong understanding of embedded system architectures, real-time operating systems (RTOS), and microcontroller programming.
- Proficiency in debugging tools and techniques for embedded environments.
- Familiarity with version control systems (e.g., Git, SVN).
- Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Effective communication and teamwork abilities.

## Other Requirements
- Experience with additional programming languages such as Python or C.
- Knowledge of hardware interfaces and protocols (I2C, SPI, UART, CAN, etc.).
- Exposure to Agile or Scrum development methodologies.
- Certifications in embedded systems or relevant technologies are a plus.
