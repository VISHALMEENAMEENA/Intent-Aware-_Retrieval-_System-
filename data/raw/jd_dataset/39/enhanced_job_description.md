## Job Title
AWS Cloud Consultant

## Location
N/A

## Client Industry
Life Sciences and Healthcare

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Design and implement AWS serverless architectures tailored to the needs of the Life Sciences and Healthcare industry.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to understand business requirements and translate them into technical solutions.
- Provide guidance and best practices for cloud security, compliance, and data protection in a healthcare context.
- Conduct regular assessments and optimizations of existing cloud infrastructure to ensure peak performance and cost-efficiency.
- Mentor and support junior staff in AWS cloud technologies and practices.
- Contribute to the development of strategic technology roadmaps and ensure alignment with industry trends and regulatory requirements.

## Skill Requirements
- A minimum of 10 years of experience in cloud computing with a strong focus on AWS technologies.
- Proven expertise in deploying and managing serverless architectures.
- Strong understanding of AWS services such as Lambda, API Gateway, DynamoDB, and S3.
- Experience in the Life Sciences and Healthcare sector is preferred.
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
- AWS Certified Solutions Architect or equivalent certification is highly desirable.
- Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to work independently and collaboratively.

## Other Requirements
- Familiarity with healthcare regulations such as HIPAA and GDPR.
- Experience with DevOps practices and automation tools.
- Strong communication skills and the ability to convey technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders.
