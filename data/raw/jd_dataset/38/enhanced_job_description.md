## Job Title
React Developer

## Location
N/A

## Client Industry
Technology

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Develop and maintain complex web applications using React.js.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design, and ship new features.
- Optimize components for maximum performance across a vast array of web-capable devices and browsers.
- Ensure the technical feasibility of UI/UX designs.
- Write clean, scalable, and reusable code while adhering to best practices.
- Participate in code reviews to maintain code quality and share knowledge.
- Debug and resolve technical issues in a timely manner.
- Stay updated with emerging technologies and industry trends to continuously improve skills and project outcomes.

## Skill Requirements
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in web development with a strong focus on React.js.
- Proficiency in JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and related web technologies.
- Experience with state management libraries such as Redux or MobX.
- Familiarity with RESTful APIs and asynchronous request handling.
- Strong understanding of version control systems, preferably Git.
- Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Strong communication and teamwork abilities.

## Other Requirements
- Experience with TypeScript.
- Knowledge of server-side rendering and Next.js.
- Familiarity with testing frameworks such as Jest or Mocha.
- Understanding of CI/CD pipelines and DevOps practices.
- Previous experience in an Agile/Scrum development environment.
