## Job Title
Angular 15 Developer – Enterprise Integration

## Location
N/A

## Client Industry
Information Technology / Technology Solutions

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Design, develop, and maintain robust web applications using Angular 15, adhering to industry best practices and HCLTech coding standards.
- Collaborate closely with backend engineers, UI/UX designers, and business stakeholders to translate requirements into scalable and maintainable technical solutions.
- Lead and implement enterprise integration initiatives, connecting Angular applications with APIs, third-party services, and legacy systems.
- Optimize application performance, troubleshoot complex issues, and ensure cross-browser compatibility.
- Participate in code reviews, provide constructive feedback, and mentor junior developers as needed.
- Ensure all deliverables meet high standards of security, scalability, and accessibility.
- Document technical designs, integration flows, and best practices for future reference and team enablement.
- Actively contribute to Agile development processes, including sprint planning, stand-ups, and retrospectives.

## Skill Requirements
- Minimum of 5 years’ hands-on experience in front-end development, with a strong focus on Angular (including at least 1 year with Angular 15).
- Proven track record of successful enterprise integration projects, including API consumption and third-party service integration.
- Proficiency in TypeScript, JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3, and responsive design principles.
- Solid understanding of RESTful API design, asynchronous programming, and state management libraries (e.g., NgRx).
- Experience with version control systems (e.g., Git) and CI/CD pipelines.
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field (or equivalent work experience).
- Strong analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills.

## Other Requirements
- Experience with containerization (e.g., Docker), cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP), or microservices architecture.
- Familiarity with automated testing frameworks (e.g., Jasmine, Karma) and test-driven development.
- Exposure to Agile methodologies and tools such as JIRA or Azure DevOps.
- Previous experience working in a multinational or enterprise-scale environment.
