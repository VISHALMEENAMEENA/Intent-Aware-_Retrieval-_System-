## Job Title
Embedded Software Engineer (C/C++)

## Location
N/A

## Client Industry
Information Technology & Services

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Design, develop, and maintain embedded software solutions using C and C++.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams including hardware engineers, system architects, and QA specialists to define software requirements and system specifications.
- Write efficient, reliable, and well-documented code for embedded applications.
- Conduct code reviews, unit testing, and debugging to ensure robust and error-free software delivery.
- Participate in the full software development lifecycle, from concept and design to implementation and maintenance.
- Optimize and enhance existing embedded solutions to improve performance, scalability, and reliability.
- Stay abreast of emerging technologies, industry trends, and best practices in embedded systems development.
- Provide technical support and troubleshooting expertise during product deployment and post-launch phases.

## Skill Requirements
- Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Electronics, Electrical Engineering, or a related field.
- 5-6 years of hands-on experience in embedded software development using C and C++.
- Strong understanding of embedded systems architecture, RTOS concepts, and microcontroller/microprocessor-based design.
- Proficiency in debugging tools, version control systems, and code optimization techniques.
- Experience with hardware/software integration and low-level driver development.
- Solid problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Excellent communication and teamwork abilities.

## Other Requirements
- Familiarity with communication protocols (e.g., I2C, SPI, UART, CAN).
- Experience with Agile methodologies or similar software development processes.
- Exposure to safety-critical or real-time embedded systems.
- Certifications in embedded systems or related technologies are a plus.
