## Job Title
Springboot Technical Lead

## Location
Noida, India

## Client Industry
Information Technology / Multinational Technology Solutions Provider

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Lead the design, development, and implementation of RESTful web services and enterprise applications using Java/J2EE and Spring Boot.
- Define and enforce best practices for coding standards, testing, and deployment to ensure robustness, scalability, and security.
- Provide technical leadership to a team of developers, conducting code reviews, resolving complex technical challenges, and mentoring junior members.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams—including business analysts, architects, QA, and DevOps—to gather requirements and shape architectural decisions.
- Drive adoption of microservices architecture, cloud technologies, and containerization (e.g., Docker, Kubernetes) to modernize application delivery.
- Oversee and integrate DevOps practices, including CI/CD pipelines and automation using tools such as TeamCity.
- Stay current with emerging technologies, industry trends, and best practices, recommending innovations to enhance team capabilities and solution offerings.
- Ensure timely and quality delivery of projects, aligning team efforts with organizational objectives.

## Skill Requirements
- Strong expertise in Java/J2EE technologies, Spring Boot framework, and development of RESTful web services.
- Proven track record of designing, building, and deploying scalable and secure Java-based web applications.
- In-depth understanding of microservices architecture, cloud platforms, and container orchestration tools (e.g., Docker, Kubernetes).
- Hands-on experience with DevOps tools and practices, including CI/CD pipelines (preferably TeamCity).
- Familiarity with agile development methodologies and collaborative team environments.
- Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and troubleshooting skills.
- Exceptional verbal and written communication skills, with the ability to present complex technical concepts to diverse stakeholders.
- Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.

## Other Requirements
- Relevant certifications in Java, Spring Boot, cloud technologies, or DevOps practices are highly advantageous.
- Previous experience in a technical leadership or mentoring role within an agile team structure.
- Exposure to additional programming languages, frameworks, or database technologies is a plus.
