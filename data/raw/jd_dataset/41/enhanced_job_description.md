## Job Title
ServiceNow Developer

## Location
N/A

## Client Industry
Technology

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Develop and configure ServiceNow applications and modules.
- Troubleshoot, debug, and resolve issues within the ServiceNow platform.
- Translate business needs into technical solutions using ServiceNow.
- Manage end-to-end implementations, including requirements gathering, development, testing, and deployment.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure seamless integration of ServiceNow with other systems.
- Maintain and update technical documentation for ServiceNow configurations and customizations.
- Provide technical support and guidance to end-users and stakeholders.
- Ensure compliance with best practices and standards in ServiceNow development.

## Skill Requirements
- Strong understanding of the ServiceNow Platform and its core configurations.
- Proficiency in JavaScript and ServiceNow's GlideAPIs.
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
- Practical experience in managing end-to-end ServiceNow implementations.
- Good to have knowledge of AngularJS and UIB.
- Minimum of 9 years of relevant experience in ServiceNow development.
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
- ServiceNow certification is a plus.

## Other Requirements
- Experience with ServiceNow's Strategic Portfolio Management (SPM) is an added advantage.
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
- Ability to work in a fast-paced, dynamic environment.
