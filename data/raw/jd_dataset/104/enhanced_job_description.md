## Job Title
HTML/CSS Front End Developer

## Location
N/A

## Client Industry
Technology

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Develop and maintain front-end code using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
- Collaborate with UX/UI designers to translate design mockups into responsive web pages.
- Ensure the technical feasibility of UI/UX designs.
- Optimize applications for maximum speed and scalability.
- Implement and maintain best practices for web development, including code reviews and testing.
- Collaborate with back-end developers to integrate front-end and back-end components.
- Stay updated with the latest industry trends and technologies to continuously improve development processes.

## Skill Requirements
- Proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
- Experience with front-end frameworks and libraries such as Bootstrap, React, or Angular.
- Strong understanding of responsive design principles and cross-browser compatibility.
- Familiarity with version control systems like Git.
- Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
- Minimum of 3 years of experience in front-end development.

## Other Requirements
- Experience with CSS preprocessors like SASS or LESS.
- Knowledge of SEO principles and best practices.
- Familiarity with Agile/Scrum methodologies.
- Strong communication and teamwork skills.
