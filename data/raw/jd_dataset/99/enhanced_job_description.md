## Location

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Design and architect advanced AI and ML solutions to address complex business challenges.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify opportunities for AI integration and optimization.
- Lead the development and deployment of AI models and algorithms, ensuring accuracy and performance.
- Provide technical guidance and mentorship to junior AI/ML engineers.

- Minimum of 8 years of experience in technology solutions, with at least 2 years specializing in AI/ML.

- Strong understanding of machine learning algorithms and principles.
- Experience with cloud platforms and AI services (e.g., AWS, Azure, GCP).
- Excellent problem-solving skills and ability to work in a collaborative environment.
- Strong communication skills to articulate complex AI concepts to non-technical stakeholders.

## Other Requirements

- Knowledge of data engineering and big data technologies.
- Familiarity with agile development methodologies.
- Prior experience in a multinational technology provider company.
