## Detailed Responsibilities
- Provide Level 2 support for complex systems and applications, including rolling out new systems, maintaining, migrating, upgrading, and improving long-term performance.
- Support the installation, optimization, integration, troubleshooting, backup, recovery, modification, security, and upgrading of IT systems and components.
- Work with senior staff to recommend and design systems architecture and topology from both general and specific perspectives.
- Provide L3 support on a rotational on-call basis.
- Identify and recommend security patches for platforms and test their deployment.
- Engage in scripting for automation of workloads and routine system admin tasks.
- Work in shifts (24*7) as part of the job responsibilities.

## Skill Requirements
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering or equivalent experience in IT.
- 7-10 years of progressive experience in support and engineering capacities.
- Strong work habits and mid-level career experience in maintaining large numbers of UNIX servers.
- Experience with administering servers and storage within Azure cloud environments and OCI (Oracle Cloud).
- Deep understanding of UNIX/Linux fundamentals and concepts.
- Extensive system administration experience with RedHat, OEL, AIX, Solaris, and other Linux flavors.
- Experience in managing VERITAS cluster servers (VCS) is a plus.
- Hands-on experience with HP and Dell bare metal server deployments.
- Proven experience in VMware running large-scale virtualized environments.
- Scripting experience with Ruby/Perl and shell scripting languages.
- Experience with Chef/Puppet/CfEngine is advantageous.
- In-depth knowledge of TCP/IP, BIND, LDAP, and other core technologies.
- Extensive troubleshooting skills in Linux and network environments in complex application and database setups.
- Strong understanding of network and server security.
