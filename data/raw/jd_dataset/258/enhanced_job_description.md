## Job Title

## Location
N/A

## Client Industry
Information Technology & Services

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Develop and maintain responsive web applications using React.js and Next.js frameworks.
- Integrate Generative AI features and APIs, ensuring seamless user experiences with advanced automation and personalization.
- Work closely with UI/UX designers to implement visually appealing and functional interfaces.
- Collaborate with backend engineers to connect front-end components with robust, scalable services.
- Optimize applications for maximum speed, scalability, and accessibility across devices and browsers.
- Participate in code reviews, troubleshooting, and performance tuning to uphold high development standards.
- Stay abreast of emerging web technologies and AI advancements, recommending best practices and improvements.
- Contribute to project planning, agile ceremonies, and documentation to ensure efficient delivery and stakeholder alignment.

## Skill Requirements
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field.
- Minimum 5 years of hands-on experience with React.js, including advanced component development, state management, and hooks.
- Strong proficiency in Next.js for server-side rendering, routing, and performance optimization.
- Proven experience integrating Generative AI technologies (such as OpenAI, GPT, or similar) into web applications.
- Solid understanding of modern JavaScript (ES6+), HTML5, CSS3, and responsive design principles.
- Familiarity with RESTful APIs, GraphQL, and asynchronous data handling.
- Experience with version control systems, preferably Git.
- Excellent problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and ability to work in a collaborative, fast-paced environment.

## Other Requirements
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP) and deploying serverless applications.
- Familiarity with testing frameworks (Jest, Cypress, etc.) and CI/CD pipelines.
- Knowledge of accessibility standards and internationalization.
- Strong communication skills and prior experience in client-facing roles.
