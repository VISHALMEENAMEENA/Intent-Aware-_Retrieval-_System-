## Job Title
Senior Software Developer

## Location
Noida, India

## Client Industry
Information Technology / Technology Solutions

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Develop, deliver, and maintain high-quality code in accordance with defined timelines, quality standards, and cost objectives.
- Support existing software features, troubleshoot and resolve bugs, and address ad-hoc client requests for enhancements or new developments.
- Collaborate closely with functional and technical teams to gather and analyze requirements, ensuring solutions are aligned with client and project needs.
- Provide end-to-end client support, including data presentation, information sharing, ticket resolution, and daily support activities such as monitoring client requirements and tracking task schedules for timely delivery.
- Create and maintain technical and process documentation in compliance with CMMI and client-specific requirements.
- Mentor and provide technical guidance to junior developers, fostering knowledge sharing and team capability development.
- Participate in direct client and internal team interactions to clarify requirements and support development objectives.
- Ensure adherence to best practices, coding standards, and continuous improvement within the development lifecycle.

## Skill Requirements
**Must Have:**
- Proven experience with COTS (Commercial Off-The-Shelf) based systems integration.
- Strong proficiency in enterprise software development and support.
- Expertise in COTS-EIS OneSuite or similar platforms.
- Excellent troubleshooting and problem-solving skills.
- Ability to work collaboratively in a dynamic, multicultural team environment.

**Education & Experience:**
- Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Engineering, or a related discipline.
- Several years of professional software development experience, preferably in large-scale enterprise environments.

## Other Requirements
- Exposure to CMMI process documentation and compliance.
- Strong communication and presentation skills for client-facing roles.
- Experience in mentoring or leading junior developers.
- Familiarity with Agile/Scrum methodologies.
