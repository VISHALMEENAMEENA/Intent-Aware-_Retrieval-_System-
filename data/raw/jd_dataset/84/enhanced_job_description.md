## Job Title
C++ Embedded Developer

## Location
N/A

## Client Industry
Information Technology Services / Multinational Technology Provider

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Design, develop, and optimize embedded software components using C++ for various hardware platforms.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including hardware engineers, testers, and product managers, to define requirements and deliver innovative solutions.
- Participate in code reviews, debugging, and troubleshooting to ensure high-quality, reliable software releases.
- Develop and maintain technical documentation, including software design specifications and user guides.
- Perform unit testing, integration testing, and participate in system validation activities.
- Contribute to continuous improvement initiatives by suggesting enhancements and adopting best practices in embedded systems development.
- Stay updated with the latest advancements in embedded technologies and C++ standards.

## Skill Requirements
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Electronics Engineering, Electrical Engineering, or a related field.
- Minimum of 2 years of hands-on experience in C++ programming for embedded systems.
- Strong understanding of embedded software development lifecycle, real-time operating systems (RTOS), and hardware-software integration.
- Experience with debugging tools, version control systems (e.g., Git), and performance analysis.
- Familiarity with communication protocols (e.g., UART, SPI, I2C) and microcontroller architectures.
- Excellent problem-solving skills, attention to detail, and the ability to work both independently and as part of a team.

## Other Requirements
- Experience with embedded Linux or RTOS environments is a plus.
- Knowledge of safety-critical or automotive software standards (e.g., MISRA C++, ISO 26262) is advantageous.
- Prior exposure to Agile development methodologies.
- Strong communication and interpersonal skills.
