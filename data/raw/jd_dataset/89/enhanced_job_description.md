## Job Title
Data Entry Operator

## Location
N/A

## Client Industry
Information Technology Services

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Input, update, and verify data in the company’s systems with high accuracy and speed.
- Maintain and organize electronic and paper records to ensure data integrity and confidentiality.
- Perform routine quality checks to identify and correct errors in entered data.
- Collaborate with internal teams to resolve data discrepancies and clarify information.
- Prepare and generate periodic data reports as required by management.
- Adhere to established data entry procedures and company policies.
- Support ad hoc administrative tasks related to data management as needed.

## Skill Requirements
- Proficient typing skills with a minimum of 40 words per minute (WPM).
- Fluency in English and Hindi, both written and spoken, is mandatory.
- Strong attention to detail and high level of accuracy in data processing.
- Basic knowledge of Microsoft Office Suite (Excel, Word) and data entry software.
- Minimum educational qualification: High School Diploma or equivalent.
- Previous experience in a data entry or administrative role is an advantage.
- Ability to work independently and as part of a diverse team.

## Other Requirements
- Familiarity with industry-specific data management systems.
- Certification in data entry or office administration is a plus.
- Willingness to work flexible hours or shifts as per project requirements.
