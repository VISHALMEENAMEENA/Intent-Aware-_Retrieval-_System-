## Job Title
Senior Project Manager

## Location
Noida, India

## Client Industry
Information Technology & Services

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Lead the management and operational excellence of Cisco Voice network environments, leveraging expert knowledge of Cisco Unified Communications Manager (CUCM), Unity, and related technologies to maximize system performance and service availability.
- Oversee incident, problem, and change management processes using Cisco tools and ITIL frameworks, ensuring swift resolution and minimal service interruptions.
- Mentor and guide technical support teams in troubleshooting advanced voice network issues, implementing upgrades, and deploying configuration changes across Cisco IOS and collaboration platforms.
- Drive continuous improvement by analyzing operational metrics, recommending, and implementing automation and process enhancements to optimize Cisco Voice environments.
- Engage directly with clients to interpret complex technical requirements, ensure service expectations are met, and deliver tailored solutions using Cisco voice and network management systems.
- Provide expert-level escalation support for critical incidents, coordinating with cross-functional teams to resolve systemic issues and implement preventive measures.
- Develop and maintain comprehensive documentation for network voice configurations, operational procedures, and client-specific requirements to facilitate knowledge transfer and uphold compliance standards.

## Skill Requirements
- Proven expertise in managing and troubleshooting large-scale Cisco Voice networks, including hands-on experience with Cisco Unified Communications Manager (CUCM), Unity, and Cisco IOS.
- Advanced proficiency in network management and monitoring tools specific to Cisco Voice environments.
- Strong familiarity with ITIL-based operations, service management best practices, and incident/problem/change management processes.
- Demonstrated leadership abilities in mentoring technical teams and fostering a culture of operational excellence.
- Exceptional communication skills for effective client engagement and cross-team collaboration.
- In-depth understanding of security practices and compliance considerations in voice network operations.
- Must have: Proficiency in Cisco Customer Voice Portal (CVP).

## Other Requirements
- Experience with automation and scripting in network environments is a plus.
- Familiarity with additional Cisco collaboration platforms and emerging voice technologies will be advantageous.
- Exposure to managing global client engagements and multicultural teams is beneficial.
