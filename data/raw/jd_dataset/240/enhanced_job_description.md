## Location
N/A

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Lead the development of interactive and responsive eCommerce platforms using React.
- Oversee the integration of CMS and payment gateway solutions into existing and new platforms.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather and define project requirements and specifications.
- Mentor and guide junior developers, fostering a collaborative and high-performance team environment.

- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field.
- At least 5 years of experience in front-end development with a strong emphasis on React.
- Proven expertise in developing eCommerce frontends, CMS integrations, and payment gateway implementations.
- Strong understanding of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and related front-end technologies.
- Excellent problem-solving skills and the ability to think critically and strategically.
- Effective communication and leadership skills to manage team performance and project expectations.

## Other Requirements
- Experience with agile development methodologies.
- Knowledge of backend technologies like Node.js or Express is a plus.
- Familiarity with cloud platforms such as AWS or Azure.
- Strong portfolio showcasing previous projects and accomplishments in front-end development.
