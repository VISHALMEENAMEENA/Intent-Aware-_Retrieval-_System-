## Job Title
Automation Test Engineer

## Location
Noida

## Client Industry
Technology

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Conduct continuous testing for security, APIs, and regression suites to ensure robust software performance.
- Develop and implement automation strategies, creating automated scripts and supporting data and environment configurations.
- Engage in code reviews and defect reporting to drive continuous improvements in the testing process.
- Collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams to ensure quality throughout the application lifecycle.
- Develop and execute comprehensive test plans, cases, and scripts to deliver high-quality software solutions.

## Skill Requirements
- Minimum of 5 years of experience in functional and automation testing.
- Proficiency in creating automated test scripts using C# and the EasyRepro framework.
- Demonstrated experience in Agile methodologies and DevOps practices.
- Proven ability to ensure delivery within agreed timeframes and scope.
- Strong understanding of software testing methodologies and processes.
- Experience in creating and executing test plans, test cases, and test scripts.

## Other Requirements
- Background in Microsoft Dynamics CRM functional and automation testing is advantageous but not mandatory.
