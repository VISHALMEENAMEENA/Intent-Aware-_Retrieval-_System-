## Detailed Responsibilities
- Provide technical leadership and significant contributions to product development projects and technology development efforts.
- Contribute to all aspects of product development activities and maintain comprehensive Design History Files, including requirements documentation, prototyping, technical risk mitigation, verification testing, design validation, risk management, and regulatory submissions.
- Develop engineering solutions and investigate competing technologies.
- Evaluate and identify design solutions that meet product performance, cost targets, and manufacturability requirements.
- Develop product verification and validation testing requirements, author testing protocols, and ensure products meet established specifications.
- Apply statistical techniques to ensure exceptional product quality, reliability, and robustness.
- Ensure product and performance specifications are accurate and appropriate.
- Support internal and external suppliers and manufacturers, including external design and development partners.
- Assist marketing in creating technical brochures, training programs, and technical presentations for customers and the sales force.
- Support engineering leadership as required.

## Skill Requirements
**Required:**
- Bachelor’s degree in mechanical engineering (preferably from reputed institutions like IITs/NITs/Equivalent).
- 5-15 years of related experience, or minimum 5 years of experience in an OEM of medical devices with MS, or 2 years with PhD.
- Working knowledge of FDA/EUMDR Design Controls.

**Preferred:**
- Experience in medical device development and/or sustaining engineering efforts utilizing a stage-gate process.
- Experience in developing handheld electro-mechanical devices.
- Proficiency in applying FDA/EUMDR Design Controls and international standards (e.g., 820-CFR, ISO14971, ISO10993, IEC60601, GMP).
- Ability to develop deliverables required to document product design for high volume manufacturing with high quality.
- Proficiency in Microsoft Windows Office applications including MS Outlook, PowerPoint, Project, and Visio. Working knowledge of Solidworks/CREO/UG NX.
- Expertise in creating and critically reviewing engineering drawings, tolerancing, and tolerance analysis.
- Knowledge of common manufacturing methodologies, technologies, and materials used in medical devices.

## Other Requirements
- Experience in statistical analysis and quality control.
- Strong project management skills and ability to work under tight deadlines.
- Excellent communication and presentation skills.
