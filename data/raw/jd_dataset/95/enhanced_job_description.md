## Job Title
Flutter Developer (Front End)

## Location
N/A

## Client Industry
Information Technology / Multinational Technology Solutions

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Develop, test, and deploy robust mobile and web applications using Flutter and Dart.
- Design and implement responsive user interfaces with a strong focus on usability, leveraging HTML and CSS as needed.
- Utilize Flutter widgets to create visually appealing and highly functional UI components.
- Collaborate with UX/UI designers, backend developers, and stakeholders to define and deliver project requirements.
- Participate actively in all phases of the SDLC, including requirements analysis, design, coding, testing, deployment, and maintenance.
- Write clean, efficient, and well-documented code following industry best practices and HCLTech’s coding standards.
- Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues, perform code reviews, and provide constructive feedback to peers.
- Stay up-to-date with emerging trends and advancements in Flutter, front-end development, and related technologies.
- Contribute to continuous improvement initiatives to enhance product quality and team productivity.

## Skill Requirements
- Proficient in Flutter and Dart for mobile and web application development.
- Strong understanding of HTML and CSS for front-end design and layout.
- Experience in building and managing reusable Flutter widgets.
- Solid grasp of SDLC principles and agile methodologies.
- Familiarity with Java programming is an advantage.
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field (or equivalent experience).
- Minimum 2 years of relevant experience in front-end or Flutter development.
- Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
- Strong communication and collaboration abilities.

## Other Requirements
- Experience with RESTful APIs and backend integration.
- Knowledge of state management solutions (Provider, Bloc, Redux, etc.) in Flutter.
- Familiarity with version control systems such as Git.
- Exposure to automated testing and CI/CD pipelines.
- Previous work in an Agile/Scrum environment.
- Published apps on the Apple App Store or Google Play Store is a plus.
