## Job Title
Administrator (Support & Operations)

## Location
Pune, India

## Client Industry
Information Technology & Services

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Independently resolve IT support tickets within agreed SLAs, ensuring timely and effective solutions.
- Provide on-call support for escalated incidents, demonstrating ownership and urgency.
- Conduct root cause analysis for recurring issues to drive incident reduction and process improvements.
- Maintain and update the internal knowledge base to enhance team capability and service quality.
- Mentor and train new team members and analysts, fostering a collaborative and high-performing environment.
- Adhere to quality standards, regulatory requirements, and company policies in all operational activities.
- Ensure a positive customer experience by achieving high First Call Resolution rates and minimizing ticket reopens or rejected resolutions.
- Participate in value-adding initiatives, such as process optimization and cross-functional projects.

## Skill Requirements
**Must Have:**
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
- Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities.
- Ability to work independently and manage multiple priorities.
- Commitment to delivering high-quality customer service.
- Flexibility to work in night shifts and on-call rotations.

**Educational Qualifications:**
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field (preferred).

**Experience:**
- Prior experience in IT support, operations, or a similar role is advantageous but not mandatory.

## Other Requirements
- Experience with ticketing tools and ITSM platforms (e.g., ServiceNow, Remedy) is a plus.
- Certifications such as ITIL Foundation or similar are beneficial.
- Exposure to knowledge management and process improvement initiatives is desirable.
