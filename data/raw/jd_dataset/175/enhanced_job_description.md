## Job Title
Technical Architect

## Location
Noida, India

## Client Industry
Technology and IT Services (Multinational Technology Provider)

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Collaborate with stakeholders to gather and define technical requirements for diverse software projects.
- Architect high-level system designs and provide strategic guidance through the entire software development lifecycle.
- Lead, mentor, and support development teams, ensuring adherence to best practices, coding standards, and quality guidelines.
- Evaluate, recommend, and implement tools, frameworks, and technologies to optimize software development processes.
- Conduct code reviews, troubleshoot complex issues, and perform performance optimization to ensure robust, reliable systems.
- Integrate front-end solutions with RESTful APIs and backend services.
- Stay current with the latest technological trends in JavaScript and React, proposing innovative approaches to solve business challenges.
- Document architectural decisions, maintain technical documentation, and facilitate effective communication across cross-functional teams.

## Skill Requirements
- Proficiency in JavaScript and React frameworks.
- Strong foundation in software architecture and design principles.
- Demonstrated experience designing and developing scalable, performant web applications.
- Solid working knowledge of HTML, CSS, and other front-end technologies.
- Experience integrating RESTful APIs and backend systems.
- Excellent problem-solving, analytical, and troubleshooting skills.
- Strong verbal and written communication abilities for effective cross-team collaboration.
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related technical field (or equivalent practical experience).

## Other Requirements
- AWS Certified Solutions Architect – Associate or similar cloud certification is a plus.
- Experience with documentation and technical writing.
- Exposure to cloud-native architectures and modern DevOps practices is advantageous.
