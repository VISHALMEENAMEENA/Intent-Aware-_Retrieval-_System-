## Job Title
Senior ERP Specialist

## Location
Oman

## Client Industry
Technology Solutions Provider

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Design, develop, and deploy robust ERP applications leveraging JavaScript, React.js, and Java technologies.
- Lead the technical solutioning process, ensuring applications meet performance, scalability, and quality standards.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including business analysts, project managers, and QA engineers, to define, design, and launch new features.
- Troubleshoot and resolve complex technical issues, ensuring minimal disruption to business processes.
- Mentor and provide technical guidance to junior developers, fostering a culture of continuous learning and improvement.
- Conduct code reviews and enforce adherence to coding standards and best practices.
- Integrate web applications with database systems and ensure seamless data flow within the ERP ecosystem.
- Stay abreast of emerging trends in web development and ERP technologies to recommend innovative solutions.

## Skill Requirements
- Advanced proficiency in JavaScript, React.js, and Java programming languages.
- Strong understanding of both front-end and back-end application development.
- Demonstrated experience in building and deploying web applications using modern JavaScript frameworks, particularly React.js.
- Solid knowledge of database systems and experience integrating databases with enterprise applications.
- Familiarity with agile development methodologies and project management practices.
- Excellent problem-solving, analytical, and troubleshooting skills.
- Strong communication and collaboration abilities, with experience working in multicultural teams.
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
- Relevant certifications in JavaScript, React.js, or Java are highly desirable.

## Other Requirements
- Experience with cloud-based ERP solutions or SaaS platforms is an advantage.
- Exposure to DevOps practices, CI/CD pipelines, and automated testing frameworks.
- Knowledge of additional programming languages or technologies relevant to ERP systems.
