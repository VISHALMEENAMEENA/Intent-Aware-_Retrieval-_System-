## Job Title
ServiceNow Engineer

## Location
N/A

## Client Industry
Information Technology / Multinational Technology Services

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Design, develop, and configure ServiceNow applications and modules to support business processes.
- Collaborate with business analysts, project managers, and stakeholders to gather requirements and translate them into technical solutions.
- Customize ServiceNow forms, workflows, and interfaces to enhance user experience and operational efficiency.
- Integrate ServiceNow with external systems and third-party applications.
- Troubleshoot, debug, and resolve issues related to ServiceNow implementations and performance.
- Maintain system documentation, technical specifications, and user guides.
- Ensure adherence to ITIL processes and compliance with organizational standards.
- Support platform upgrades, patching, and ongoing maintenance activities.
- Provide guidance and mentorship to junior engineers and technical support staff.
- Participate in Agile development cycles, contributing to sprint planning and review sessions.

## Skill Requirements
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field.
- Proven experience in ServiceNow development, configuration, and administration.
- Strong knowledge of ServiceNow modules such as ITSM, ITOM, HRSD, or CSM.
- Proficiency in JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and REST/SOAP integrations.
- Familiarity with ITIL framework and best practices.
- ServiceNow Certified System Administrator (CSA) or higher certification(s) required.
- Excellent problem-solving abilities and strong communication skills.
- Experience working in Agile development environments.

## Other Requirements
- ServiceNow Certified Implementation Specialist certification(s) preferred.
- Experience with ServiceNow Discovery, Orchestration, or Security Operations (SecOps) modules.
- Prior experience in multinational or large-scale enterprise environments.
- Knowledge of additional programming languages (e.g., Python) is a plus.
- Strong analytical and documentation skills.
