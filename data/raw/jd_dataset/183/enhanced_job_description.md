## Job Title
ServiceNow Architect (HRSD and PSM)

## Location
N/A

## Client Industry
Multinational Technology Provider

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Design and develop comprehensive ServiceNow solutions tailored to HRSD and PSM requirements.
- Lead and manage ServiceNow platform upgrades and version migrations.
- Implement integrations with third-party systems using APIs and web services.
- Maintain and optimize the ServiceNow CMDB and asset management processes.
- Collaborate with stakeholders to understand business needs and translate them into technical solutions.
- Provide expert guidance on best practices for ServiceNow development and architecture.
- Troubleshoot and resolve issues, ensuring system reliability and performance.
- Conduct regular code reviews and ensure adherence to coding standards.
- Mentor and support junior developers, fostering a collaborative and innovative team environment.

## Skill Requirements
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
- 12+ years of experience in ServiceNow development and architecture.
- Proficiency in HRSD, PSM, ITSM, ITOM, CMDB, and Discovery best practices.
- Strong knowledge of JavaScript, XML, HTML, and CSS for ServiceNow development.
- Experience in ServiceNow platform upgrades and version migrations.
- Ability to design and implement ServiceNow integrations with third-party systems using APIs and web services.
- Knowledge of ServiceNow CMDB and asset management.
- Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills.
- Strong communication and stakeholder management skills.
- ServiceNow Certified Implementation Specialist (CIS) certifications.

## Other Requirements
- Experience with Agile and DevOps methodologies.
- Familiarity with ITIL processes and frameworks.
- Prior experience in a multinational company setting.
