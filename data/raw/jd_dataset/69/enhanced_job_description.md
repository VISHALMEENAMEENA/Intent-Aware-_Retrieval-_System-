## Job Title
Senior Backend Developer (Python/FastAPI)

## Location
N/A

## Client Industry
Information Technology / Multinational Technology Solutions

## Detailed Responsibilities
- Design, develop, and maintain backend services and APIs using Python and FastAPI, ensuring high reliability and scalability.
- Architect and implement database models and queries using SQLAlchemy with PostgreSQL for optimal performance and data integrity.
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams including frontend developers, product managers, and QA engineers to deliver end-to-end solutions.
- Optimize application performance, troubleshoot issues, and implement effective solutions to complex technical challenges.
- Write clean, maintainable, and well-documented code following industry best practices and company standards.
- Participate in code reviews, mentor junior developers, and contribute to continuous improvement initiatives.
- Ensure security best practices are followed in all backend implementations and data handling.
- Stay abreast of emerging technologies and recommend innovative approaches to enhance system capabilities.

## Skill Requirements
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
- Minimum of 5 years of hands-on experience in backend development with Python.
- Proven expertise in FastAPI framework.
- Strong proficiency in SQLAlchemy and PostgreSQL database design and optimization.
- Solid understanding of RESTful API design principles and microservices architecture.
- Experience with version control systems (e.g., Git) and collaborative development workflows.
- Excellent problem-solving skills and a keen attention to detail.
- Strong communication and teamwork abilities.

## Other Requirements
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP) is an advantage.
- Familiarity with containerization technologies such as Docker and Kubernetes.
- Knowledge of CI/CD pipelines and DevOps practices.
- Prior experience in Agile/Scrum development environments.
- Contributions to open-source projects or relevant technical communities.
